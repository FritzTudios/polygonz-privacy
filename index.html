Privacy Policy for Polygonz

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how Polygonz handles data in connection with advertising and third‑party services. Polygonz displays interstitial and rewarded advertisements using Google AdMob.

Advertising and Data Collection

Polygonz uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may automatically collect certain information from your device, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Android advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (inferred from network information)
  • App activity related to ad interactions (such as impressions and clicks)
  • Diagnostic information used for fraud prevention and service improvement

This information may be used for purposes such as ad personalization, analytics, and detecting invalid traffic. AdMob may share aggregated or pseudonymized data with its advertising partners as described in Google’s Privacy & Terms.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Personalized vs. Non‑Personalized Ads

Depending on your region and device settings, you may receive personalized or non‑personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by adjusting your device’s advertising settings or by visiting Google’s ad personalization controls:

https://www.google.com/settings/ads

EEA/UK Consent

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom, Polygonz uses a consent management platform to request permission for personalized advertising. If consent is not granted, only non‑personalized ads will be shown.

Children’s Privacy

Polygonz is not directed toward children under 13. The app follows the target‑audience settings configured in Google Play Console and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Polygonz does not collect or store personal data directly. Any data collected by AdMob is handled according to Google’s Privacy & Terms. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game files.
